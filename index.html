Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our services.

What design services do you offer?

We offer a variety of creative design services, including Flyer Design, Brochure Design, Business Cards, Brand Kits, Resume Design, and Poster Design.

How long does a project typically take?

The timeline for each project depends on its scope. Generally, smaller projects like business cards may take 1 day, while larger projects like brand kits may take several days.

Do you provide ongoing support for design projects?

Yes, we offer ongoing support and revisions for our design projects to ensure you are satisfied with the final product.

What is your pricing model for design services?

Our pricing varies based on the project scope and requirements. We provide detailed quotes after discussing your needs for services like flyer or poster design.

Can I request custom designs?

Absolutely! We specialize in custom designs tailored to your specific needs. Just let us know your ideas, and we’ll bring them to life.

What files will I receive upon project completion?

You will receive all final design files in various formats (PDF, PNG, JPG, etc.) suitable for both print and digital use, depending on the service.

How do you handle revisions?

We typically include a set number of revisions in our projects. After that, additional revisions may incur a fee, but we aim to get it right within the initial rounds.

Do you offer branding services?

Yes, we provide comprehensive branding services, including logo design, brand strategy, and brand kits to help establish a cohesive brand identity.
